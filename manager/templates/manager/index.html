Formula Manager Game

Join the free formula racing world!

The Formula Manager game is an online strategy game where you take the role of a team manager with one goal - the top of the racing world. You are in charge of all aspects of team management: buying and selling drivers, deciding what skills to train and develop, preparing a strategy for each race in your championship, managing car research and developement.

Your races will be simulated by our system, and you can follow the action in our live commentary viewer or analyze the race afterwards. A big part of the excitement is that it is a multiplayer experience - the teams you face are managed by other humans who will try to outsmart you. You can also join a community to learn about the best ways to train, set up your team strategy, and improve your team financially. Unlike many other games, you can play at your own pace, and there is no way to buy in-game advantages. Welcome to the formula manager game - the best free strategy game out there!

Features

Manage team and drivers

Formula Manager is a strategic racing game where you build and manage your own Formula One team at your own pace. Whether you log in every day or once a week, the choices you make will determine your team's success. With the right decisions and expert management, you can lead your team to become the Champion!

Develop and train your team

Develop your own team by training your drivers and developing your car. Manage your team's finances carefully to make smart investments in research and development. Scout for new drivers or nurture young talents through your own racing academy to build the next generation of champions.

Strategize your way to victory

You compete against other human managers in racing leagues. Start at the bottom and work your way up through the ranks to become the ultimate team. Stay ahead of the curve by outmaneuvering your opponents with your superior strategy and tactics. Challenge your friends to see who has what it takes to be the best formula manager.
